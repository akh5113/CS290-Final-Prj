Why Oregon State Computer Science?

I would reccommend the Oregon State Postbaccalaureate program to anyone interested in pursuing a Computer Science degree. This is my first time taking a completely online course and it has gone better than I could have ever imagined. It takes a few weeks to get into a groove, but between the support from the online community of students, and the high-quality content of the courses, there are many ways to succeed.

Courses Taken:

  • CS 161 - Introduction to Computer Science I
  • CS 162 - Introduction to Computer Science II
  • CS 225 - Descrete Structures in Computer Science
  • CS 261 - Data Sructures
  • CS 271 - Computer Archetiture Assembly Language
  • CS 290 - Web Development
